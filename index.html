What is color Therapy?


Color therapy, also known as chromotherapy,is a form of therapy that usees color and light to treat certain mental and physical health condition.We can trace this form of therapy back to the ancient Egyptians.They made use of sun-filled rooms with colored glasses for the therapeutic purposes.


Types of color Therapy


In color Therapy, different colors are thought to be able to treat varipus conditions.Colo therapists believe the lack of a particular color in your body might be responsible for your living condition. Her's a list of the most commom colors used during therapy and how we can use them.


  • Red is used to energize or invigorate a person who might be feeling tired or down however,red may also trigger people who might already be tense,which is why it's used carefully in color therapy.
  • Blue: Chromatherapists use blue to treat depression and pain.Darker shades of blue are also thought of sedative properties and maybe helpful for people who experience insomnia or other sleeping disorders.
  • Green:Green is the color of nature,and according to chromatherapists,it can help relieve stress and relax a person.
  • Yellow:Yellow can be used to improve your mood and make you happy and optimistic.
  • Orange:Orange, much like yellow,cna be used to elicit happy emotions from people.The bright warm color is also thought ti be able to stimulate appetite and mentals activity.

Color therapy has a host of uses.There's some evidence to suggest that it can help with some of the following conditions.
